B2B Lead Intelligence

Lead Scoring & Topic Intelligence

Industry: Digital Media & Event Marketing
The Problem

Sponsors were provided with raw, flat lead sheets lacking content engagement details, which led to poor sales conversion rates and threatened critical renewals.

The Solution

Built a real-time behavioral data pipeline using AWS Glue and Snowflake. Integrated an NLP-based topic extraction model to map user clicks/reads, feeding a custom lead-scoring engine.

Impact Details

Processed 1M+ lead profiles. Sponsors saw a 30% increase in sales conversion rates by pitching precise interests. Retained all major event sponsorship revenues.

GenAI / Document Automation

GenAI Report & Document Automation

Industry: Public Sector & Local Government
The Problem

Case managers spent 15-20 hours manually drafting each Education, Health, and Care Plan (EHCP) from fragmented physical documents, causing a massive processing backlog.

The Solution

Created "EHCP Plus," a GenAI multi-agent workflow. Used AWS Textract for OCR and Whisper for dictation. Programmed automated retrieval (RAG), section drafting, and multi-agent citation check.

Impact Details

Reduced document drafting time by 80% (under 3 hours per plan). Cleared months of historical case backlog within 90 days, easing operational burnout.

Computer Vision / Brand Safety

Automated Media Monitoring System

Industry: Media & Intellectual Property Management
The Problem

Scammers hijacked executive likenesses for deepfake crypto scams on video platforms. Manual searches were too slow, allowing unauthorized content to sit online for days.

The Solution

Developed an automated scanner using Selenium, frame-sampling, and a fine-tuned Python facial recognition model. Deployed on AWS ECS/Fargate with Slack/webhook alerts.

Impact Details

Achieved 100% automation. Cut alert response time from 48 hours to under 15 minutes, reducing manual review costs by 95% and securing high-profile clients.
